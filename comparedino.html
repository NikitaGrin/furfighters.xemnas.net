Visual Comparisons - Dinotopolis

This page lists differences between the levels and Fluffmatches found within Dinotopolis.

Dinos Upstairs


An additional set of shelves were added.


The duvet/comforter was changed from a Fur Fighters one to a random pattern. No idea why.


An animated (...) poster of General Viggo was added. Check it out.


A Juliette Telepoint was added, along with a rather familiar magazine.


A new parody poster was added.


Sergeant Sternhauser's LP cover was added.


A door was added, likely to make it harder for Tweek to reach his Flea level.


The scales were flipped and the switch was moved to the front, likely to make it easier to spot.


A whole new area was added in Viggo's Revenge, containing the exclusive Flamethrower weapon. Juliette cannot reach this place in other versions, and even if she could - There's nothing more than a wall there.

The Rumpus Room


Some stairs were removed. In Viggo's Revenge, you automatically return to the top upon completion of your task, and the area is sealed off.


A shelf was added with various equipment and some Viggo Industries paint cans.


An iron and ironing board were added. A Bungalow Telepoint has been added underneath the sink.
