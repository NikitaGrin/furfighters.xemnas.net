Visual Comparisons - Cape Canardo

This page lists differences between the levels and Fluffmatches found within Cape Canardo.

Fluff Resurrection


The ramp is now a moving platform instead. The bears seen above have actually been moved in Viggo's Revenge, and aren't quite as rampant in the level. A sound clip was also removed from this area.


Even looking in, the entrance looks drastically different, since so much of this level has been revamped.


This gigantic statue of General Viggo with a very 'Xenomorph' body was completely removed in Viggo's Revenge, and replaced with a hallway of bears in containers.


So yeah. Computer room got a HUGE update.


The bottom of the computer room looks much more detailed, and leads off to two different places on the bottom floor as opposed to just one in the original.


These pockets were replaced with windows and a different floor texture/ground.


Although this place is mostly forgettable in the PC version, Viggo's Revenge has moved the bears over here instead.


There are two ways to go here, but in Viggo's Revenge, only one path is ahead of you.


These two areas are completely absent from Viggo's Revenge, with no similar area to be found. Although the bears are used in the hallway, the giant computer is nowhere in this fluffmatch.
